# Cee Ess Designs

The spoke for the Cee Ess Designs build: a Melbourne graphic design studio site. Live on a custom
`.com.au` domain with a full brand portfolio, animated UI, contact forms, and SEO. DA's featured
website case study on the marketing site.

- **Governed by:** [company-brief](../brain/company-brief.md), [tone-of-voice](../brain/tone-of-voice.md),

## Surfaces

- **URL:** cee-essdesigns.com.au (custom domain, Vercel, HTTPS by default).
- **Repo / deploy:** its own Vercel project (source not in this repo).

## How it was built

- Hand-coded static site, mobile-first, with animated loading and a custom cursor.
- **Full SEO suite:** Open Graph, Twitter cards, JSON-LD structured data.
- **Forms:** Formspree integration with a service-type selector.
- **Content:** 5 brand case studies in the portfolio.

## Scope

In: the marketing site, portfolio case studies, contact form, SEO, custom domain. Out: ongoing
content updates unless retained.
