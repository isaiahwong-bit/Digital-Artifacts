# Lead Response

## The promise

Every lead answered in minutes, in your voice, and nothing slips through. You see why each reply
went out, you own the system that sends it, and you can change how it works any time.

## Who it is for

A small Australian business (5 to 50 staff) where enquiries arrive faster than one person can keep
up, and the first reply too often lands hours or days late, or never.

## The problem

Most AI tools promise to handle this, then drift. They forget your offer, invent details, and you
cannot see why they said what they said. So you stop trusting them and go back to doing it by hand.
The data backs the frustration: most AI projects stall, not because the model is weak, but because
generic tools never learn your business. And speed is money: a lead contacted within 5 minutes is
8 times more likely to convert than one contacted after 30. Slow, inconsistent first replies are
quietly costing you work.

## What you get

A small system that does one job well:
- Watches your inbox and flags the real leads the moment they land.
- Drafts the first reply and the follow-ups in your voice, from a brain that holds your offer,
  your tone, and your rules.
- Waits for your yes before anything sends. You approve, edit, or skip.
- Logs every decision, so you can always see what went out and why.

## How it works, and why it does not drift

The truth lives in a brain you own: plain files that hold who you are, what you sell, and how you
talk. The AI reads the brain, it never rewrites it. You correct a fact once and every future reply
is right. Nothing reaches a customer without a human saying yes. This is the opposite of a
black-box prompt.

## You own it

The whole thing is yours from day one: the brain, the automations, the logins. No subscription that
dies if you cancel, no lock-in. I set it up with you and show you how to extend it, so you can add
to it yourself. When you want more, you come back. You are not renting a dependency.

## The proof

I run this on my own studio. It watches my inbox and drafts my outreach right now. You are buying
something I use every day, not a demo.

## What it costs

- Setup: A$5,000 to A$12,000, fixed price, scoped on a call. Where you land depends on how many
  channels and follow-up steps you want.
- Optional support: A$500 to A$2,500 per month, only if you want me on call to extend it and keep
  it healthy. Cancel any time. Most start without it and add it later.
- No hourly billing, no surprises.

## What it is not

- Not another SaaS subscription stacked on your bill.
- Not a locked box you cannot see into or take with you.
- Not a fragile prompt that breaks the first time your offer changes.
