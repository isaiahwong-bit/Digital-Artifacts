# Who we are

Digital Artifacts is a boutique Australian studio that builds AI operating systems for small business. We are not a typical automation agency; we deploy a reusable 4-layer framework (Context, Skills, Automation, Agent) for whatever function a business needs to run itself.

Single-operator studio. Direct work, no account managers, no outsourced delivery. Based in Australia; work remote across AU and UK.

# What we sell

1. **Initial 4-layer deploy** — one-time engagement, 4-8 weeks. Sets up the brain (Layer 1), 3-5 named skills (Layer 2), 2-4 n8n workflows (Layer 3), and one surface (Layer 4 — Slack / SMS / voice / dashboard).
2. **Brain + skills growth retainer** — monthly. Monitoring, new skills, new automations, grooming the brain as the business changes.
3. **Websites** — Layer 4 rendered as a front-end. Marketing sites, client portals, internal dashboards, job-tracking tools.

# The governing rule

Humans write, AI reads. The Layer 1 brain is only edited by humans through a review step. The AI reads it at runtime but never writes to it. Correct a fact once, every future output is right.

# What makes us different

- The 4-layer framework separates truth (in Git) from reasoning (in the model). Most AI tools collapse both into one opaque prompt.
- Outputs are traceable. Every brief, every draft cites the brain file it came from.
- You own the stack. Repo, n8n workflows, surfaces: all handed over. Not locked into a vendor.
- Swap Claude for another model tomorrow; the brain doesn't change.

# Non-negotiables

- No em dashes anywhere in client-facing copy.
- Direct, warm, peer-to-peer tone. Never salesy. Never corporate.
- Every inbound lead gets a response within one business day, same-day if during business hours.
- Every AI-written output has a human reviewing before it ships.
