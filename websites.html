04 Questions

Before you ask.

How long does a site take?
Most sites go live in about 14 days from kickoff. Bigger builds with a CMS or booking run a little longer. You get a clear timeline before any money changes hands.
Do you write the content?
We shape and tighten what you have, and write the structure and the SEO copy. If you are starting from a blank page, we draft it together. You sign off on every word.
Will it rank on Google?
Every site ships with the on-page basics done properly: titles, meta, structured data, a sitemap, and fast hosting. Ranking over time also depends on your reviews and fresh content, which we can help you keep up.
What if I need changes later?
A care plan covers ongoing tweaks and updates, or you can edit the site yourself. Either way you are never stuck waiting.
Who owns the site?
You do. The code, the domain, and the content are yours, with no lock-in. Hosting is on Vercel under your own account if you want it that way.
Can you automate the back end too?
Yes. A website is Layer 4 of a bigger system. We can wire the quote form, the follow-ups, the review requests, and more behind it. See the automation page.
